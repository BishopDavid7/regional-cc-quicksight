<h2>Executive Summary</h2>
<p>
This story explores how professor effectiveness (<em>EvaluationScore</em>) relates to course costs and enrollment. 
We identify high-performing professors who achieve excellent evaluation scores at moderate or low cost, and highlight 
low-cost courses with poor evaluations that would benefit most from targeted interventions.
</p>
