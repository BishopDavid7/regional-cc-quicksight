<h2>Executive Summary</h2>
<p>
This story explores how professor effectiveness (<em>EvaluationScore</em>) relates to course costs and enrollment. 
We identify best practice professors who earn high evaluation scores at moderate or low cost, and low-cost courses with poor evaluations 
that would benefit most from targeted interventions.
</p>

<h2>Recommendations</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Pilot an instructor development program focused on identified low-cost, low-eval courses.</li>
    <li>Document and scale teaching practices from high-eval/low-cost professors.</li>
    <li>Use QuickSight with weekly refreshes to monitor impact.</li>
</ul>
